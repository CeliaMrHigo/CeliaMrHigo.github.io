FAQs

Some questions about us

Who are we?

We are the union of several families dedicated to agriculture, who want to make known the extra value of their products and we believe that together is better.

Why Mr. Higo?

Because trust, quality and service are hard to find, and we have it.

Why are we here?

Because we are the third generation of farmers in our family and we not only want our products to be known, but also the care and effort we put into each one of them.

What do we want?

Our main objective is to satisfy the expectations of an increasingly demanding market, with a product to which we dedicate 100% of our time, in order to be the best travelling companion.

Have we got bio?

Of course! We currently have several products in our catalogue that we grow both conventionally and organically. You can write to us at info@mrhigo.com and we will send you our catalogue of organic products.

What is our guarantee?

Both we and our farmers are GFSI-certified for our agricultural practices and certified for good social practices. This ensures that our work is 100% quality.
